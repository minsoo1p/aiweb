Initial diagnosis

AutoAngle aids in the initial diagnosis by providing precise measurements of critical angles in x-ray images, helping physicians identify orthopedic conditions accurately. Its automated analysis reduces the time needed for diagnosis, allowing for quicker patient care decisions.

Serial follow-up

For outpatient management, AutoAngle facilitates serial follow-up by consistently tracking and comparing changes in x-ray images over time. This ensures that physicians can monitor disease progression or recovery with reliable, repeatable accuracy.

Postoperative evaluation

After surgery, AutoAngle offers valuable insights during postoperative evaluations by accurately assessing the alignment of bones. This enables physicians to make informed decisions about patient recovery and any necessary further interventions.

Information

Learn about the innovative journey behind AutoAngle and discover how to seamlessly integrate it into your orthopedic imaging workflow.

AutoAngle was born out of a collaborative project at a bootcamp hosted by the Research of Doctor Physician Programmers under KoSAIM. This project brought together medical students and specialists, forming a team dedicated to advancing orthopedic imaging analysis through technology and innovation.

Using cutting-edge computer vision and deep learning techniques, the team trained the system to accurately measure critical angles in x-ray images. This process involved developing a proprietary algorithm capable of delivering precise and reliable results, revolutionizing the way orthopedic conditions are diagnosed and managed.

User guide

  1. Sign Up and Log In

    To access the AutoAngle service, you must first sign up and log in.

  2. Create a Project

    Start by creating a new project for your analysis.

  3. Select Site and X-ray Type, Then Upload Files

    Choose the anatomical site and type of x-ray, then upload the relevant files.

  4. Enter Processing Mode

    Enter processing mode to begin analyzing the uploaded files.

  5. Review and modify Angles for Each Image, Then Confirm

    Check the measured angles for each x-ray image, make adjustments if necessary, and click the confirm button.

  6. Export the Data as an Excel File

    Export the final data in Excel format for your records.
